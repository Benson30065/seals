Seal profiles

Niko the Seal

Species: Baikal seal (native to Lake Baikal, Russia). Born at Toba Aquarium in Japan.

Known for: Viral, human-like expressions. Also nicknamed “the cement eater.”

Vibe: Expressive, meme-worthy, instantly recognizable.

Yo-chan

Background: Rescue seal at the Tokari Center.

Known for: Silly expressions and the “OUT OF MY WAY” meme moment.

Vibe: Chaotic cool with comedic timing.

Yuki

Popularity: One of the most popular seals (often right behind Niko).

Known for: Legendary ROUNDNESS and being a common plush design.

Vibe: Maximum cuteness, minimal edges.
